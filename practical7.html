Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India & People's President

About

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs and inspired millions through his vision for a developed India.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Key figure in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna
  • Served as President of India (2002–2007)

Inspiration

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."

His life continues to inspire students, scientists, and citizens to work towards innovation, leadership, and national development.
